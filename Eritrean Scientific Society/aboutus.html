Design and Layout

My name is Abel Mehari, age 27 years old. I am currently working in the design and layout of this website and building of the EriSci android application. I graduated in 2017 with a Bachelor of Science in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Mai-Nefhi Eritrean Institute of Technology (EIT).


Why I Started this Blog?


The project of creating an application and website designing came as an idea before two years, after the release of the magazine named Mentor Megazine stopped from releasing, where I used to participate there in the design and layout for one year. I really appreciate the devotion and dream of the mentor volunteer group, which aims to help students in their studies and help them follow their passion. So, as we don't want the dream to fade, we are starting this webpage and application, so that we all Eritreans share our knowledge and help each other.


The Message


We don't have to wait to be great and start sharing, we just have to start sharing to be great. Since we all have something to share, so we really look your participation in making this blog a useful page to all Eritreans who are following their studies and seeking helps. So please send your articles you think they are important to be shared and projects.
